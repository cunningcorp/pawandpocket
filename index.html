How this is calculated

Figures are US-market averages for routine years, scaled by size, food tier, and grooming. The first year adds one-time setup (spay/neuter, initial vaccines, microchip, starter gear). Lifetime multiplies a typical year by an average lifespan for the size.

These are planning estimates, not vendor quotes — your real costs depend on your vet, location, and dog. Always check with your vet for medical decisions.

On currency: the toggle converts the US estimate at today's rate. It does not re-price for UK or EU vet and insurance costs, which differ — a future version would use region-specific data.
